# Privacy Policy

**Last Updated: April 22, 2026**

This Privacy Policy describes how Anygram ("we," "us," or "our") collects, uses, and shares your personal information when you use our mobile application and web services. By using Anygram, you agree to the terms outlined in this policy.

## 1. Information We Collect

We collect information to provide a better experience, synchronize your data, and manage your subscription tiers.

### a. Account Information
When you create an account or sign in, we collect:
*   **Identifiers:** Your name, email address, and a unique user ID.
*   **Profile Data:** Your profile picture (if provided via third-party sign-in).

### b. User-Generated Content
Anygram is designed to help you create and manage diagrams. We store:
*   **Diagram Syntax:** The Mermaid.js code generated or edited by you.
*   **Metadata:** Diagram titles, timestamps, and relationship hierarchy (parent/child branches).
*   **Source Text:** Any prompts or text snippets you provide to generate diagrams.

### c. Document Processing
If you utilize our document-to-diagram feature, we process:
*   **Extracted Text:** Content extracted from PDF, DOCX, or text files you upload. This text is processed temporarily to generate your diagram.

### d. Subscription and Payment Data
To manage your "Pro" status, we process:
*   **Subscription Status:** Whether you have an active monthly or yearly plan.
*   **Purchase History:** Transaction identifiers provided by the platform (e.g., Google Play Store).
*   **Note:** We do not collect or store your credit card details directly; these are handled by our payment processing partners.

### e. Usage Statistics
We track your usage (e.g., number of generations, uploads, and refinements performed) to enforce service tier boundaries. This data is synchronized with our secure cloud servers and associated with your account to ensure consistent service limits across your devices and maintain service integrity.

## 2. How We Use Your Information

We use the collected data for the following purposes:
*   **Service Provision:** To generate, render, and save your diagrams.
*   **Cloud Synchronization:** To allow you to access your diagrams and consistent usage limits across multiple devices when signed in.
*   **Account Management:** To verify your identity and manage your subscription level.
*   **Usage Enforcement:** To manage and enforce fair-use limits and subscription tier boundaries.
*   **AI Processing:** To convert your text or documents into structured diagrams using secure AI processing services.
*   **Product Improvement:** To analyze general usage patterns (anonymized) to improve our generation algorithms.

## 3. Data Storage and Security

*   **Local Storage:** Many of your preferences and diagrams are stored locally on your device for offline access.
*   **Cloud Infrastructure:** If you are signed in, your diagrams and account profile are stored on secure cloud servers.
*   **Security Measures:** We implement industry-standard encryption for data in transit and at rest to protect your personal information from unauthorized access.

## 4. Third-Party Services

We partner with third-party providers to facilitate specific features. These providers are strictly vetted and are not permitted to use your data for any purpose other than providing the service to Anygram.

*   **Authentication Providers:** Used to manage secure login (e.g., Google Sign-In).
*   **AI Service Providers:** Your prompts and extracted text are sent to AI models to generate diagram syntax. These providers do not use your data to train their models.
*   **Subscription Management:** Used to verify your purchase status and manage recurring billing via the app store.
*   **Cloud Infrastructure:** Used to host our backend services and database.

## 5. Data Retention and Deletion

*   **Retention:** We retain your account and diagram data as long as your account is active.
*   **User Control:** You can delete individual diagrams at any time within the app.
*   **Account Deletion:** You may request full account deletion through the application settings. Upon deletion, all your cloud-stored diagrams and account information will be permanently removed from our production servers.

## 6. Age Limitations and Protection of Minors

Anygram is strictly intended for individuals who are at least 18 years of age. We do not knowingly collect, solicit, or maintain personal information from individuals under the age of 18. If we become aware that we have inadvertently collected personal data from a minor under 18, we will take immediate steps to delete such information from our records.

## 7. Changes to This Policy

We may update our Privacy Policy from time to time. We will notify you of any changes by posting the new Privacy Policy on this page and updating the "Last Updated" date.

If you have any questions about this Privacy Policy or our data practices, please contact us through the application settings or the official support channels.
